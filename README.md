# Global-Sports-Footwear-Sales-Dashboard
An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing global sports footwear sales from 2018 to 2026, highlighting revenue trends, top-performing brands, sales channels, and product segmentation to deliver actionable business insights.

# ğŸ¯ Dashboard Objectives
This dashboard was created to analyze global footwear sales and identify key performance indicators (KPIs). Specifically, it aims to answer the following questions:
- What is the total revenue from 2018 to 2026?
- How many products were sold during this period?
- What is the average customer rating (1.0 being the lowest and 5.0 being the highest)?
- What percentage of sales came from online channels?
- Which year recorded the highest sales?
- Which brand generated the highest revenue?
- Which shoe category generated the highest revenue?
- Which product gender has the highest market share?
- Do customers prefer purchasing online or in retail stores?
